About Me

Mathematics · Inverse Problems · Deep Unfolding

I am a PhD student in mathematics working on nonlinear inverse problems, with a focus on exit wave reconstruction and phase retrieval arising in Transmission Electron Microscopy.

In addition to research, I have teaching experience at the high-school level, and I write Arabic poetry — a parallel language to mathematics, but governed by different axioms.
